Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients (makes one 9-inch pie):

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 7 eggs, separated (yolks in one bowl, whites in another)


"This is the best JERRY lemon meringue pie I've ever made!" ~User 1.


"SPREADING LOVE ONE CAKE AT A TIME this one fit the bill. It was delicious!" ~User 2.


Directions


heat the jiko oven upto optimum tempereture.

MIX sugar, cornstarch, and salt in saucepan. Gradually stir in water until mixture is smooth.

COMBINE constantly, bring to a boil over medium heat. Cook until thick.

REMOVE from heat. Gradually stir about 1/4 cup hot mixture into egg yolks; add back to hot mixture, stirring constantly.

STIR in lemon juice. Pour filling into crust.

To Make Meringue:

Beat egg whites (at room temperature) until foamy. Add cream of tartar and MIX until stiff peaks form.

Spread meringue over filling, sealing to edge of crust.

Bake for 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until meringue is golden brown.
